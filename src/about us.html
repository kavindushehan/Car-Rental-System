ABOUT US

With the experience in the industry, we strive to offer the highest levels of customer service and a highly personalised service to all our customers.

With one of the largest and most modern and varied fleets in Sri Lanka, our service is backed by a networked front office, fully-fledged mechanical servicing and valet servicing onsite making us one of the preferred providers of Sri Lanka car rentals in the island.

Our Clients

Our loyal clientele consist of the largest and the most diverse segments comprising individuals, companies and groups who make use of our vehicle rental services for all types of purposes. Our customer base include individual customers both local & foreign visiting Sri Lanka for business & leisure as well as government & nongovernmental organisations, corporate and diplomatic missions. We also serve hotels, airlines, travel agents and tour operators. We have earned many commendations, accolades as well as received endorsements & compliments from both organisations and individual customers for our relentless, untiring effort as a company to better the services offered.

Our Fleet

Our fleet of vehicles is the largest ever offered in the car rental service in Sri Lanka. With a fleet of over 500 vehicles which include cars, vans, double cabs, SUV’s, coaches and luxury vehicles such as Chrysler, Hummer, Benz, and BMW, we also boast the latest addition to our fleet, the 24 foot Chrysler limousine which symbolises the pinnacle of luxury and class, pioneering the limousine service in Sri Lanka. Our wide range of vehicles offered for transportation needs consists of over 50 4WD jeeps, 50 Vans, 200 cars and double cabs and buses.

Trust and Reliability

Make an online booking with a deposit and be certain that a vehicle of your choice will be assigned to you on the requested date and time. No excuses. Period.

Convenience

When you pick up your vehicle, paperwork is kept to a minimum. Fill out the essentials and you are on your way. Hassle free to the max.

Safety

Choose from our fleet of latest vehicles with the best safety features. All vehicles are equipped with driver and passenger airbags, seatbelts for all passengers and side collision bars on the doors and much more.
